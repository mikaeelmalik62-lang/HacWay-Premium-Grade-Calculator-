# 📊 GradeHub Pro — HAC Grade Tracker

A clean, fast, browser-based grade tracker built for middle and high school students using the **HAC (Home Access Center)** grading system. No login. No app. Just open it and go.

🌐 **Live Site:** [mikaeelmalik62-lang.github.io/HacWay-Premium-Grade-Calculator-](https://mikaeelmalik62-lang.github.io/HacWay-Premium-Grade-Calculator-)

---

## ✨ Features

- **Add your full schedule** — enter each class with its name, period, and grade weights
- **Track assignments** — log grades by category (Knowledge & Thinking, Agency, Collaboration)
- **Edit grades on the fly** — inline editing so you never have to delete and re-add
- **Live GPA & averages** — automatically calculates your weighted average and 4.0 GPA as you add grades
- **What-If Predictor** — see how a future grade would affect your overall average before you get it
- **Grade Trends chart** — visual bar chart comparing all your classes at a glance
- **Category Breakdown** — see how you're doing in each grading category per class
- **Everything saves automatically** — your data stays in your browser, no account needed

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Visit the live site above
2. Click **"＋ Add a Class"** in the sidebar to set up your schedule
3. Enter your class name, period, and grade weights from your syllabus (they must add up to 100%)
4. Click a class → **"+ Add Assignment"** to start logging grades
5. Watch your GPA update in real time!

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Pure **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** — zero dependencies, zero frameworks
- Grades are stored in your browser's **localStorage** (private to your device)
- Hosted for free on **GitHub Pages**

---

## 📌 Notes

- This tracker is designed for schools that use the **K&T / Agency / Collaboration** weighted grading system
- Since data is stored in localStorage, it's **per-browser** — clearing your browser data will reset it
- Works best on desktop
